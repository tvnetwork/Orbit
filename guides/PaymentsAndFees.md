# Payments & Fees Guide

Learn how payments, escrow, and platform fees work on VyntaJobs.

## Key Points
- All payments are held in secure escrow until milestones are approved.
- Supported payment methods: credit card, PayPal, bank transfer.
- Platform fee: 10% on successful projects.
- Withdrawals: Go to Wallet > Withdraw Funds.

For more, see the Wallet and Contracts guides.
